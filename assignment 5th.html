My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Inception
    • Year: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page
    • Description: A skilled thief who steals corporate secrets through the use of dream-sharing technology is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can implant an idea into a target's subconscious.
  2. Interstellar
    • Year: 2014
    • Main Actors: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain
    • Description: A team of explorers travel through a wormhole in space in an attempt to ensure humanity's survival as Earth faces a global food crisis.
  3. The Shawshank Redemption
    • Year: 1994
    • Main Actors: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman
    • Description: Two imprisoned men bond over a number of years, finding solace and eventual redemption through acts of common decency.
  4. Baahubali
    • Year: 2015
    • Main Actors: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty
    • Description: A young man learns about his royal heritage and sets out on a mission to rescue his mother and reclaim his kingdom from an evil king.
  5. Deadpool & Wolverine
    • Year: 2024
    • Main Actors: Ryan Reynolds, Morena Baccarin, Josh Brolin
    • Description: The wisecracking mercenary Deadpool returns for a third installment, facing off against new villains while continuing his irreverent adventures.
